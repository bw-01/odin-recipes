Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a yummy dish of Italian origin.

To make lasagna, follow the below recipe.

Ingredients

  • Pasta sheets
  • Mince meat
  • Tomato based sauce
  • Onions
  • Cheddar

Steps

  1. Cook onions
  2. Add mince
  3. Add sauce and simmer down
  4. Layer between pasta and cheese and bake in over
